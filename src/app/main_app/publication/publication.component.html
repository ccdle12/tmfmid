Publish Your Survey

Publish your survey when your team has completed the survey and after you have held a workshop to, if necessary, modify the results using 'adjust aggregates'. This will finalize the survey and you will be able to view this via the benchmark page. Your selected level of openness will determine whether you can also view other organization's results as well as the global benchmark. Following publication your organization will be readied for a new survey and all user's data will be returned to a starting position.
